# Aquarium World - Game Design Document

## 1. High Concept

Aquarium World is a relaxing aquarium simulation game where players build, maintain and expand their dream aquarium.

The game is designed to run smoothly on low-end PCs and Android devices.

The philosophy is:

"Performance first. Gameplay first."

---

## 2. Genre

- Aquarium Simulator
- Tycoon
- Management
- Casual + Simulation (Hybrid)

---

## 3. Target Audience

- Aquarium lovers
- Casual players
- Mobile gamers
- PC players with low-end hardware
- Players aged 10+

---

## 4. Core Gameplay Loop

1. Feed fish
2. Maintain water quality
3. Earn coins
4. Buy fish
5. Decorate aquarium
6. Increase Reputation
7. Unlock new content
